Paw and Order Homepage

Paw and Order helps pet parents effectively train their dogs, leading to a happier life for both humans and pups. Yearly, they work with roughly 100 human clients and have a staff of 5 expert trainers who train approximately 200 dogs, along with an employee who is the office manager.
